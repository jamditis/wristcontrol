Powerful Features

Everything you need for hands-free computer control, designed for productivity and accessibility.

Gesture Control

Use natural finger pinches and hand movements to control your cursor. Tap for click, double-tap for double-click, and hold for right-click.

Voice Commands

Speak naturally to control your computer. Voice commands for clicking, scrolling, typing, and executing shortcuts.

Motion Cursor

Move your hand to move the cursor. Smooth, responsive tracking with configurable sensitivity and acceleration.

Bluetooth LE

Low-latency wireless connection to your computer. Battery-efficient streaming that won't drain your watch.

Fully Configurable

Customize sensitivity, dead zones, gesture mappings, and voice commands. Create per-application profiles.

Cross-Platform

Works on Windows, macOS, and Linux. Same great experience across all your devices.
